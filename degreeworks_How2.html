Whats in Degreeworks

At the very top you will see information about you

In degree works you will see some other subsections.

The important ones are:

-Anything with pathways

-Anything with requirements

But make sure you also pay attention to the section about your schools program's requirments

After the Aforementioned are completed you can begin applying for your major's classes

-note that you can take your major classes along side your requriment classes as long as the prerequirment classes for it are met.

What is pathways

In degree works you will see some other subsections.

Dont worry too much about the name

what you need to know is that you must complete the classes in those catagories

like in the section that says "Pathaways-flexible core requirments" and "Pathways-College option" you must complete those classes.

think of this like your general education classes.

What classes to take

As mentioned above the classes you must take and complete are the ones with requirment or pathways in it.

If you are a freshmen then you would have to take the pathways flexible core and college option classes first.

After those classes are out of the way you can begin to take your other requirment classes as required by your school and program at your school.
