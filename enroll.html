ENROLLMENT

How To Register For Classes

Step 1: Get advised.

Consult with a program/faculty adviser from the CUNY school you committed to in order to find out which classes you should enroll in.

Step 2: Enroll in classes.

Go to CUNYFirst, select "Student Center," and select "Search For Classes."

Step 3: Financial aid package selection.

You will receive a tuition charge after you enroll in classes. You will also receive a financial aid package if you applied for FAFSA. Accept your financial aid package.

Step 4: Check Degreeworks

Use Degreeworks to check the credits you have left.
